title: FXPanel for Reaper

intro: Quick-access floating panel for adding your favorite FX to tracks in REAPER — drag-and-drop layout, per-button images, and a built-in FX browser.

section!: Requirements
**ReaImGui** extension (free) — install via ReaPack: Browse packages > search _ReaImGui_ > Install > restart REAPER.

section: Features
- One-click insertion of VST, VST3, AU and CLAP plugins onto tracks
- Drag-and-drop button layout, freely resizable panel
- Per-button PNG/JPEG thumbnails
- Built-in FX browser with search and folder filtering
- Layout and images persist across REAPER restarts

section: Support
Free to use. An optional supporter license (available on Gumroad) removes the donation reminder and supports continued development.
